About me

Hi there! I'm a 21-year-old CMD (Communication and Multimedia Design) student, and before this, I spent five years at secondary school (Havo). My passion for creativity has always driven me, and design has become my favorite way to express that. When I'm not busy with school, I love drawing, gaming, watching tv-shows, listening to music and hitting the gym. I'm also picking up some Japanese, which brings a fresh cultural perspective to my work.

Graphic design, has always fascinated me, pushing me to find new ways to create engaging and intuitive experiences. I'm on a journey to become a skilled UI/UX and visual designer with front-end experience, constantly eager to learn and grow in this exciting field. Through my portfolio, I hope to share my creativity, technical skills, and love for design, crafting unique and user-friendly solutions along the way.

I've had the chance to work on several group projects, which taught me a lot about the importance of teamwork and communication. These experiences have helped me improve my design skills and approach problems from different perspectives. I believe great design is all about balancing functionality with aesthetics, and that's what I aim for in all my projects.
